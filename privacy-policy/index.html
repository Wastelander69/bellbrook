Privacy Policy

How we handle information on this demo site.

Effective date:

1) Overview

This Privacy Policy explains what information may be collected when you visit this website and how that information may be used. This site is a demo / showcase experience created for portfolio and presentation purposes.

2) Information we may collect

  • Basic usage data: device type, browser type, pages visited, approximate location (city/region), and referral source.
  • Form submissions: if you submit a form on the Contact page, you may provide your name, email, and message content.

3) How information may be used

  • To improve the website experience and performance.
  • To respond to inquiries (if the form is connected to email/service in the future).
  • To understand which resources visitors find helpful.

4) Cookies & analytics

This demo site may be configured to use cookies or analytics in the future. If enabled, cookies may be used to remember preferences and to measure website usage. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

5) Third-party content

Some pages embed third-party video content (e.g., Veteos player embeds). These providers may collect information according to their own policies. Review third-party privacy policies when applicable.

6) Data security

Reasonable safeguards may be used to protect information, but no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

7) Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us through the Contact page.
