Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers to common questions — no stress, no guessing.

Do I need an appointment?

For most visits, yes. Scheduling helps us give you dedicated time and keeps the lobby calm. If your pet has an urgent issue, call first so we can help you decide the best next step.

What should I bring to my first visit?

Any previous medical records (if you have them), a list of medications, and your questions. If your pet gets nervous, bring treats or a favorite toy for comfort.

How often does my pet need vaccines?

It depends on age, lifestyle, and risk factors. We’ll build a plan that keeps your pet protected without overdoing it.

Do you offer dental cleanings?

Yes. Dental health is a big part of overall wellness. We can evaluate your pet’s mouth and walk you through options (and what to expect).

My pet hates taking medication — what can I do?

You’re not alone. We can show you helpful techniques and suggest options like flavored meds or pill pockets when appropriate. Check our “Giving Medications” resource for tips.

Can I ask a quick question without a visit?

Often, yes — especially for general questions. For anything medical, we may recommend an exam so we can give safe, accurate guidance.

Still have questions?

Reach out — we’ll point you in the right direction.
