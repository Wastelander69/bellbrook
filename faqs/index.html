Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers to common questions — no stress, no guessing.

Do I need an appointment?

Yes — appointments help us give each pet the time and attention they deserve. If you’re unsure how urgent something is, give us a call and we’ll guide you.

What should I bring to my first visit?

Bring any prior medical records, a list of medications or supplements, and lots of treats if your pet has favorites.

My pet is nervous — what can I do?

You’re not alone. Let us know ahead of time and we can plan a calmer visit, offer happy visits, or adjust handling to keep things low-stress.

Do you offer preventive care plans?

Preventive care is tailored to each pet based on age, lifestyle, and health. Your veterinarian will recommend vaccines, parasite prevention, and wellness schedules.

How do I refill medications?

Contact us directly to request refills. Some medications require rechecks before continuing, so we’ll let you know what’s needed.

Is this a real veterinary clinic?

Still have questions?

We’re happy to help — reach out anytime.
